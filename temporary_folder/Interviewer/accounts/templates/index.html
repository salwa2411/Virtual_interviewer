About Virtual Interviewer

In a job interview one needs technical skills as well as soft skills. Many a times students don't understand why are they not able to crack an interview. So our Virtual Interviewer will guide the candidate to learn and understand the negatives and positives in himself/herself. This would be a mock interviewer for the aspiring Candidates and decrease the rate of unemployment in our country.

A virtual interviewer for students which guides them to improve their communication, body language, skills etc so that they are ready to give an interview and clear it easily.

The Candidate will be providedd with a report after interview after logging into our website

Instructions would be provided before the interview, it will be a video call interview and everything would be analyzed by the virtual interviewer.

Profile page will be containing the progress report of the candidate. It would be available after login.
